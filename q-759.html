Project Description

The brand has gone through multiple expansions and is selling more than snacks, but a whole variety of goods. But that means their positioning has also changed, and brings on major competitors like Parknshop and 7-11. They have to try hard to stand out with a distinct brand image and identity.
This project aims to improve brand identity and consistency in physical stores to enhance experience and brand image
