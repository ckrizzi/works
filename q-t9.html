Teanine

From the leaves of a tea, be in touch with yourself and your roots.


Project Description

The name Teanine relates to the nine minute experience, also from the Theanine in tea which makes people relaxed. The project aims to provide a full tea experience in 9 minutes. It targets urban workers to have a short and comfy resort in their busy work while immersing themselves in tea and new knowledge. About tea, and themselves.

Project Date 2021
